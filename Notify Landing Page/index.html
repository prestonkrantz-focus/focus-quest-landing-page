The Architecture

Three Layers of Sovereignty

01

The Launch Moment

Before the feed opens, a 5-second pause. Not a wall. A breath.

  • Vagus Gate: A slow haptic pulse—inhale, exhale. Shifts your nervous system from reflexive-seeking to calm-intent.
  • Intent Prompt: A single, non-judgmental question: "What are you looking for?" No answer required. It re-engages your prefrontal cortex.
  • Interface Proxy: Route directly to DMs or Search, bypassing the algorithmic discovery feed entirely.
02

The Session

An unbreakable active window that fades out, rather than slamming shut.

  • Valve Protocol: Set an active window (e.g., 15 minutes). No "Ignore Limit" button.
  • Sensory Fade: At 1 minute left, color saturation drains. At 0:00, the app is greyscale. The visual reward is gone, making disengagement effortless.
  • The Archive: Save any post to a queue for later. Leave without FOMO.
03

The Cooldown Window

We own the transition out of the app.

  • Somatic Reset (60s): A guided, minimal visual breathing prompt to address the emotional dysregulation that started the session.
  • Mood Check-In (10s): One tap—Happy, Okay, Stressed, Bored. Builds a personal pattern map over time.
