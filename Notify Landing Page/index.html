The real problem

You don't lack
willpower.

You open your phone when you're bored, stressed, or avoiding something. Blocking apps doesn't fix that — you just override the block. What actually works is a pause that helps your brain catch up with your hands.

Sentio gives you that pause. A short breathing moment before the app opens. A session that ends gracefully instead of abruptly. And a cooldown that fades the urge instead of fighting it.

"I'd just unblock myself. The limit wasn't the problem. I was the problem."

— SMU student interview, research phase

How Sentio is different
→

→

→

How it works

Three layers.
One protocol.

01 — Before you open
Pause before
you scroll.

Every time you open a tracked app, Sentio plays a short breathing moment with a gentle haptic pulse. It's not a lecture — it's five seconds that help you choose instead of react.

+ Skip straight to DMs or Following instead of the main feed
02 — While you're in
A timer, not
a blocker.

You get a window of time (15 min by default) to use the app like normal. When it's almost up, the screen gently fades to grey. At zero, apps close on their own. No "Ignore Limit" button.

+ Gradual greyscale fade · + Can't be bypassed
03 — After it ends
A cooldown that
actually works.

After your session, Sentio gives you a 30-minute cooldown. You'll get a quick mood check-in and a breathing moment. The urge to open the app again usually fades in about 20 minutes.

+ Breathing animation · + Mood tracking · + Apps return automatically
Features

Simple by design.
Effective by default.

No complicated setup. No screens full of toggles. Sentio does one thing: helps you stop scrolling when you didn't mean to start.

01
Enforced sessions

When your session ends, apps actually close. No "Ignore Limit" button, no workaround. They come back automatically after your cooldown — no willpower needed.

02
Greyscale fade

In the last minute of your session, the screen gradually drains to grey. It's a quiet visual cue — your brain registers "this is ending" without a single alert or popup.

03
Smart app launch

Open Instagram straight to DMs. Open Twitter to your Following feed. Skip the algorithm-curated discovery page entirely — go directly to what you actually opened the app for.

04
Breathing pause

Before any tracked app opens, you get a 5-second breathing moment with a gentle haptic pulse. Not a quiz, not a popup — just a beat that helps you open with intention.

05
Adaptive sessions

Each week, your session window can shorten by 2 minutes — but only when your mood data says you're ready, and only with your say-so. Progress at your own pace.

06
Your progress

See hours reclaimed, focus streaks, and mood trends. Everything on the dashboard highlights what you've gained — not what you've lost. You're not breaking a habit. You're building one.
