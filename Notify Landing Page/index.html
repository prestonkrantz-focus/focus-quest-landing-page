Your brain craves closure.
Sentio provides the ending.

We don't scroll because we lack discipline. We scroll because our nervous system is seeking regulation. Your phone is a highly effective tool to manage overstimulation or fatigue—until it traps you.

The problem isn't the screen, it's the absence of an ending. Infinite feeds exploit our psychological need for closure. Sentio artificially induces that closure, honoring your need to regulate without losing another hour to the void.

What Sentio Is Not

Not a blocker that says no.
Not a screen time tracker that makes you feel bad.
Not a dumbphone workaround.
Not a willpower app.
Not another thing that assumes you're the problem.

It gets easier. On purpose.

Sentio isn't static. It tracks your progress over weeks and gradually adjusts — shorter sessions, smarter cooldowns. Think of it like a training plan for your attention. Week one is different from month two.
