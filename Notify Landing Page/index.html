what it is

Doomscrolling isn't a willpower problem.
It's an emotional one.

You don't need a stricter app. You need somewhere to land between scrolls — a place that isn't shaming you, scoring you, or making you fight your own phone. Cove is that place.

how it works

Three quiet beats. No fight required.

i.
Step in.
A five-second breath before the app opens. Your nervous system gets a moment to catch up to your thumb.
ii.
Soft close.
Your scrolling window fades quietly to greyscale instead of slamming shut. No "Ignore Limit" button. No fight.
iii.
The cove.
A guided breath, a one-tap mood check, your saved posts waiting. The pause between scrolls — held for you.

You're not broken.
You're misconfigured.
Cove doesn't say no — it regulates.

— the cove principle
what it isn't

Less app. More you.

The screen-time category is full of products that gamify your shame. Cove deletes the scoreboard.

The category

  • Streaks, gems, leaderboards
  • $100/year for the real features
  • Friends see your screen time
  • Hard blocks you'll bypass anyway
  • Treats you like a problem

Cove

  • One feature, done warmly
  • Free forever
  • Private by design — no friends, no feed
  • Sensory fade. No fight, no override
  • Treats you like a person

Cove never sees what you scroll.

Everything stays on your device. No telemetry, no analytics, no profile. Mood and stats are yours alone, with optional iCloud sync if you want them on a second device.
