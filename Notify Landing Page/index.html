what it is

your brainrot, on a timer.

tiktok, reels, and shorts aren't designed to entertain you for two minutes — they're designed to put you in a trance. within the first fifteen minutes of scrolling, you stop choosing what to watch. you "wake up" forty-five minutes later and don't remember most of it. that's the trance. it's not a willpower problem. it's a chemical loop your phone is built to trigger. the otter ends the session before that happens.

how it works

three things. that's the app.

i.
pick the apps.
tiktok, reels, shorts, snap — whatever's cooking your brain. the otter holds the apps you choose. he doesn't ask why.
ii.
scroll for 15. then he takes it.
the screen fades to grey. the apps lock. dms stay open the whole time — cove routes you straight to your inbox.
iii.
wait 30. he claps. window opens.
no ignore button. no bypass. just the otter, floating on his back, holding your phone until your nervous system catches up.

you're not broken.
