Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 6, 2026

Sentio ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at focusquest.site and use the Sentio application.

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Name and email address when you join our waitlist
  • Device and app usage metadata required for Sentio's attention protocol (e.g., session durations, app open events)
  • Mood check-in responses you submit during cooldown windows

Information collected automatically:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • Anonymous usage analytics (page views, feature engagement)
  • We do not collect browsing history, message content, photos, or any content from your social media apps

How We Use Your Information

  • To operate Sentio's attention protocol (session timing, cooldown windows, mood tracking)
  • To personalize your experience and adapt your protocol over time
  • To communicate with you about your account and product updates
  • To improve and develop the Sentio product

Data Storage & Security

Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your personal information. Mood and session data is stored locally on your device whenever possible, with only aggregated, anonymized data sent to our servers for protocol calibration.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for research purposes. We will never share your individual mood data, session history, or app usage with third parties.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us. You may opt out of non-essential communications at any time.

Screen Time & Accessibility Permissions

Sentio requires Screen Time access (iOS) to operate the attention protocol. This permission is secured with Face ID or your device passcode. Sentio cannot read, access, or store any content from your social media apps — it only manages session timing and app-open routing.

Children's Privacy

Sentio is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at hello@focusquest.site.
